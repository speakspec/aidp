    details: DNS verification, business registration, multi-dimensional trust scores. Trust is verified, not self-declared.
  - title: Multi-format Output
    details: One AIDP source automatically produces JSON, Schema.org, llms.txt, and Open Graph. Maintain once, use everywhere.

  - title: Content Integrity
    details: Community dispute mechanism allows third parties to challenge inaccurate information, preventing outdated or incorrect data from persisting.
  - title: AI Exposure Tracking
    details: Track which AI Agents use your content, how often, and which items are most popular. Understand your visibility in the AI world.
